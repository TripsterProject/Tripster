Cuisine

Discover the treasury of taste Portorož has to offer throughout the year. Traditional Istrian kitchen, enriched with Mediterranean aromas will bring a smile to your face which is typical of local hospitality and authenticity. Try home-made food or indulge in specialities prepared by renowned chefs.

Hidden corners

Every day can be a new adventure! Go on historical paths, visit important monuments and natural attractions as well as other special attractions the surrounding area has to offer. Inspiration, combined with rich heritage and the modern offerings, is waiting for you every step of the way in Portorož.

Wellness by the sea

Submerge yourself in a pool of brine just like the Benedictine monks used to do in the 13th century. This concentrated sea water is rich in minerals, bromine, iodine and magnesium, which encourages and strengthens the body's defence mechanism and makes you look younger as it improves blood circulation and purifies your body.
