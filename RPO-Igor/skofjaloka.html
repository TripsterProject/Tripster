The Stari Vrh Ski Resort

The Stari Vrh ski resort is the main winter sports tourist centre in the Škofja Loka hills. It offers a variety of ski pistes as well as activities for non-skiers. The Snow Park boasts a toboggan run, a bobsleigh track for tubing, a small ski jumping ramp and various outdoor play equipment for children. Lovers of hiking can explore the numerous hiking trails that lead to the surrounding hilltops, villages and tourist farms, where there is the opportunity to witness the area's rich cultural, ethnological and natural heritage.

Mišouh Toboggan Run

The toboggan run is 2.5 kilometres in length, sledging is possible by day and by night and it is also suitable for children. In the evening the track is illuminated, partly by torches, with each person taking part also receiving a head lamp. The toboggan run is well-arranged and maintained and exposed areas are protected with wooden fencing or snow banks.

Astonishing town

In beautiful, mystical, medieval Škofja Loka, inspiration from the past can be used to create new memories for the future. The town is home to the Škofja Loka Passion Play, the oldest Slovenian drama. Every six years you too can become a part of its staging! Škofja Loka is on the confluence of two rivers and has a special light and energy, which, for centuries, has been attracting painters and other artists.
