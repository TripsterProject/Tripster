Horse tour across the Senozeta Hills

Horse riding to the nearby hill is appropriate for beginners and experienced riders. When we ride past the hayracks and the Senožeta Ski Centre, we can enjoy in magnificent views stretching towards the lower and upper valleys of Bohinj. At the top of the Senožeta hills, we can admire the lower Bohinj mountains and the highest Slovenian mountain, Mt Triglav.

Bohinjski flyer

Simulation device of ski flights over 250 m, first of a kind in Slovenia. It is used for safe training of ski jumps for young athletes, and at the same times alows everyone else to experience feeling of jumping champions and helping them overcome their fears and limitations.

Magical Lake

The panoramic views of Lake Bohinj and the surrounding hills awaken our senses in all seasons, and enjoying in these views from the back of a horse is even more unique.
